The Basics of Scrum

  • Scrum is a process that allows teams to build products incrementally in iterations called sprints.
  • It enables teams to focus on delivering the highest business value in the shortest time.
  • The Scrum team evaluates the development daily in stand-up meetings (Daily Scrum).
